### Solutions
#### Brute Force
```py
class Solution:
    def findMaxK(self, nums: List[int]) -> int:
        
        res = -1
        
        for i in nums:
            for j in nums:
                if -i == j:
                    res = max(res, i)
        return res
```
##### Time & Space Complexity
* Time complexity: $O(n^2)$
* Space complexity: $O(1)$
